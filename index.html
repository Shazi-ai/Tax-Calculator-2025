About Our Tax Calculator

Understanding how we calculate your taxes

Tax Calculation Methodology

Our 2025 tax calculator provides an estimate of your federal income tax based on projected tax brackets, deduction amounts, and credits for the 2025 tax year.

How We Calculate Your Taxes

  1. We start by adding up all your income sources (wages, dividends, interest, capital gains).
  2. Next, we subtract your deductions (either standard or itemized).
  3. The result is your taxable income, which is taxed progressively based on income brackets.
  4. We then apply any tax credits to reduce your total tax liability.

The calculator includes the standard deduction for different filing statuses, projected for 2025. For simplicity, our basic calculator treats capital gains as ordinary income. Tax credits directly reduce your tax liability and can significantly lower your final tax bill.
